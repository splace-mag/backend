Read, turn & play!

splace is a playful communication medium designed specifically for reading on tablets. Depending on how the tablet is used (vertically or horizontally), different versions of the articles are being displayed. The full version is only available for tablets.

On closer examination different interpretation of the concept of colour emerge. We sent out questions on colour to students and professors of the University of Art and Design Linz and got a colourful palette of answers in return.

With selected works of students produced in the past academic year, the splace gallery offers a representative insight into various study areas, levels of education and thereby provides an overview of the artistic potential at the University of Art and Design Linz. The selection criteria to be presented in the splace gallery are the choice of media, strength of concept and a thematic link to the current issue.

The white \splace gallery with selected works by students.

With splace – a project of the University of Art and Design Linz – both students and their lecturers explore the scope of new digital communication formats for publications, exhibitions and various media. The playful magazine explores current positions and topics in the field of art, culture and society. Content, design and usability are specially designed for reading on tablets.
